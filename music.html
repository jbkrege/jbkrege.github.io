History
I have studied music since I was six years old, when I began taking violin lessons. When I was thirteen I began playing piano. In middle and high school I played bass in the orchetra. By the age of sixteen I had taught myself ukulele and guitar as well. As a senior in high school I auditioned to be part of the bass performance program at Northwestern and was accepted. Currently I am studying Music Technology at Northwestern.
