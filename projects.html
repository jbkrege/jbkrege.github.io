Laser Harp

I am particularly excited about this project because it lies at the intersection of two of my most driving interests, music and design. I'm working with Northwestern's chapter of IEEE to develop a laser harp that combines the functionality of a laser harp with that of a LaunchPad, an array of buttons commonly used by electronic musicians, to turn a performance effect into a versitile instrument for live performance.
